Micro vs. Meso — a quick contrast

Understand why more people are exploring a deeper practice.

Micro Dosing

  • Works quietly in the background
  • Popular wellness trend
  • Tiny shifts, often unnoticed
  • Minimal disruption, minimal change

Meso Dosing

  • Intentional sessions with clear framing
  • Creates time for reflection and integration
  • Invites clarity, balance, and connection
  • Supported by journaling, community, and rhythm

The rhythm of practice

A simple session might include

  1. Taking your dose in a quiet morning moment
  2. Journaling what arises — thoughts, sensations, emotions
  3. Moving your body (walk, breathwork, stretching)
  4. Returning to your day with a clearer mind

It’s not complicated. The key is noticing what shifts when you give yourself space.
